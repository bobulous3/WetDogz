Meet the Band:

Jiffwards "Cha Cha Cha" Crossline

Tip-toeing through the mists of ‘ROCK’, this Crossline’s monumental past transcends nothing but myth and musicianship.

He’s a chancer – he’s a prancer – by Jove he’s a disco-dancer! And this may well be the peak performance from the legend we know as Jiffy Crossline: Cha Cha Cha!

It all began when he was found out as ‘too good a drummer to get away with it’ as Jiffy describes: “I always pissed off the guitar players by being too good, so my first band: The RockDocktors stuck me on the skins.

Well I was good at that too – and that kind of sealed the deal! I progressed to Glam-Rock as a Fish-Head with the fabled Maaad Cod at the helm. Now I’m bashing sticks with JSPXL!” Not a word of lie spoken Jiffy.

This from a man that can tinkle the ivories with aplomb, slap the bass with gusto and pluck the acoustic with virtuosity! And the talent doesn’t end there – given the dance floor, Jiffy can slink out a quickstep or foxtrot his toes off all night long! It goes without saying that his favoured dance is the Cha Cha Cha. As Jiffy says himself: “Its just as Del La Soul said..... 3 – its the magic number
